### Issues & Recommendations

#### Social SEO Issues:
*   **Duplicate Social Previews:** Because OG tags are hardcoded in the static HTML shell, sharing a link to `swiftmart.space/shops` or `swiftmart.space/products` on WhatsApp, Slack, Facebook, or Twitter will display the homepage logo and description instead of shop/product details.
*   *Recommendation:* Implement dynamic SSR (Server-Side Rendering) or use pre-rendering tools (like Vite SSG or Prerender.io) so that crawlers scrape distinct OG tags for each route.

#### Local SEO Issues:
*   **Lack of Local Citations:** The complete absence of physical office addresses and business contact numbers prevents Google from connecting your website to Google Maps and Google Business Profile (GBP) listings.
*   *Recommendation:* Add a global page footer that clearly states the physical headquarters address, support email, phone number, and operating hours.
