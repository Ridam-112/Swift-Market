---

### Key Recommendations

*   **Implement Trust Pages:** The most critical trust gap is the lack of legal and contact pages. If a customer has a billing issue or wants to check returns, the absence of support pages will cause them to abandon the purchase.
*   **Enhance Product Detail Social Proof:** Displaying a rating of `4.8` without any written customer context feels artificial. Adding short, real text reviews will dramatically boost conversion rates.

---

### Top 10 CRO Improvements (Ranked by Conversion Impact)

1.  **Add a "WhatsApp Chat Support" Button (High Impact)**  
    *Action:* Embed a sticky floating WhatsApp icon in the bottom corner of the mobile layout to resolve order disputes or delivery questions instantly.
2.  **Highlight a First-Order Discount Code (High Impact)**  
    *Action:* Place a coupon banner above the fold on the home page (e.g., "Use code FIRST50 for free delivery on your first order").
3.  **Add a Sticky "Add to Cart" Button on Mobile Product Pages (High Impact)**  
    *Action:* When scrolling down to read product details on mobile, display a sticky bottom bar with the "Add to Cart" button so users don't have to scroll back up to buy.
4.  **Display "Low Stock" Warnings (Medium Impact)**  
    *Action:* Add dynamic urgency indicators (e.g., "Only 3 left in stock!") on product cards when stock drops below 5 items.
5.  **List Real Customer Reviews on Product Pages (Medium Impact)**  
    *Action:* Expand the product detail page schema to show 2-3 recent text reviews below the description.
6.  **Add a Local Food Safety Compliance Badge (Medium Impact)**  
    *Action:* Display standard regulatory symbols (like FSSAI compliance logos) on vendor pages to reassure users about hygiene.
7.  **Create a Dedicated Support Page with Click-to-Call (Medium Impact)**  
    *Action:* Resolve the 404 on `/contact-support` by building a simple page featuring a click-to-call phone link.
8.  **Introduce "Frequently Bought Together" Bundles (Medium Impact)**  
    *Action:* On the product page, bundle related items (e.g. Milk + Bread) with a single button to add both to the cart.
9.  **Add Payment Provider Logos in Cart/Checkout (Low Impact)**  
    *Action:* Add visual trust icons for Razorpay, UPI, Google Pay, and Visa during checkout to reassure users about transaction safety.
10. **Implement a "Recently Viewed" Section (Low Impact)**  
    *Action:* Add a swipeable row of recently viewed items at the bottom of the home page to ease re-navigation.
